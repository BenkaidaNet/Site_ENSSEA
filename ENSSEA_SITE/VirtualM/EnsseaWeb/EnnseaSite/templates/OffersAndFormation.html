{% blocktrans %} The National Higher School of Statistics and Applied Economics provides graduation and post-graduation courses, which cover Three higher education cycles : {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'First cycle: Preparatory Classes' %}
{% trans 'CONDITIONS OF ACCESS' %}

{% blocktrans %} the School annually receives new graduates oriented towards preparatory classes, according to the orientation conditions set by the Ministry of higher education and scientific research, namely : The wishes expressed by the new bachelors ; The results obtained at the baccalaureate ; The reception capacities of higher schools. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Second cycle: Master's degree {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans 'CONDITIONS OF ACCESS' %}

{% blocktrans %} the School receives new students from all preparatory schools of Economic, Commercial and Management Sciences, at the end of a national competition for access to higher schools, organized at the end of the first cycle. This competition is open to students who have been declared admitted at the end of the second preparatory year. Admitted students can pursue three (03) years of training, i.e. one (01) year of joint education (02 semesters) and two years of specialty (04 semesters). The last semester is entirely devoted to the internship and the preparation of the final thesis, which is publicly defended in front of a jury. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Third cycle: Doctorate' %}
{% trans 'CONDITIONS OF ACCESS' %}

{% blocktrans %} Registration for the Doctorate is open to students holding a Master's degree or equivalent (or state engineer) in the field of statistics and applied economics, according to the conditions set by the various doctoral training offers of the School. The candidates participate in a national competition according to the conditions of the School and those, selected at the end of this competition, will have the choice between several specialties. {% endblocktrans %}
