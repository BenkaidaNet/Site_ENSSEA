{% blocktrans %} Two years during which the basic knowledge is taught for mastery by the student before moving on to the second cycle.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Students who pass the contest enter the first year of the second cycle.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'TWO YEARS OF SPECIALTY (4TH + 5TH YEAR)' %}

{% blocktrans %}The School offers its students six specialties which are taught for the engineering cycle (Statistics and Data Science, Applied Statistics, Applied Economics and Prospective, Statistics and Economic Prospective, Finance and Actuarial Science, Population Sciences and Statistics Social).
