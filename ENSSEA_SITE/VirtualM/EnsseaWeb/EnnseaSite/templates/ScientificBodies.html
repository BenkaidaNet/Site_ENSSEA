{% blocktrans %} The Scientific Council is an advisory body which gives its opinion and proposes guidelines for the scientific and pedagogical affairs of the School; it is chaired by the Director of the School composed of Deputy Directors, heads of Departments, presidents of scientific committees of departments, Directors of research laboratories, the Director of the Library, permanent teachers reporting to other higher education institutions and elected representatives of teachers of the School.
The Scientific Committee of the Department is an advisory structure that issues opinions and recommendations on scientific and pedagogical affairs submitted to it by the head of department; it includes, in addition to the head of Department, elected Representatives of the teachers of the Department. {% endblocktrans %}
